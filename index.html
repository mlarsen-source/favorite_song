Stairway to Heaven by Led Zeppelin

Composed by the band's guitarist Jimmy Page with lyrics written by lead singer Robert Plant, "Stairway to Heaven" is widely regarded as one of the greatest rock songs of all time. Released on 8 November 1971 on the band's untitled fourth studio album by Atlantic Records. Although the song was released in 1971, it continues to top lists of the greatest rock songs today.
